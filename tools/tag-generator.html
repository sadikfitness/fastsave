❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many tags should I add to a YouTube video?+
YouTube allows up to 500 characters for tags. Use 30-40 relevant tags that accurately describe your video. Mix broad keywords (youtube tips) with specific ones (youtube tips for beginners 2024).
Do YouTube tags still help with SEO in 2024?+
Yes! While YouTube's algorithm has evolved, tags remain important for helping YouTube categorize your content and match your video to relevant search queries. They're especially important for misspellings of your keywords.
Is the Tag Generator completely free?+
100% free, always! No account needed, no daily limits, no hidden fees. Use it as many times as you want. FastSave.me is committed to providing free tools for all creators.
What's the best tag strategy?+
Start with your exact video title as the first tag. Then add variations with keywords, related topics, and your channel name. Avoid unrelated tags — YouTube can penalize this. Our generator follows all best practices automatically.
