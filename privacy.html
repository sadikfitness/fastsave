Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to FastSave.me. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our YouTube tools.

2. Data We Collect

FastSave.me is a client-side application. This means most of your data (like API keys and video URLs) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not store your YouTube API keys on our servers.

  • API Keys: Stored locally on your device only.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our tools.

3. YouTube API Services

Our application uses YouTube API Services. By using our tools, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Any data you enter into our tools is processed in real-time. Results generated (like tags, descriptions, or articles) are for your personal use and are not saved by FastSave.me unless you explicitly download or copy them.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fastsave.me.
