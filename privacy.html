Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FastSave.me. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our free YouTube creator tools at fastsave.me.

2. Data We Collect

FastSave.me is primarily a client-side application. Most processing happens in your browser, not on our servers.

  • YouTube API Keys: Stored only in your browser's localStorage. Never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Video/Channel URLs: Entered by you and used only for the specific tool request. Not stored.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized usage data (pages visited, tools used, browser type, country). No personally identifiable information is collected. IP addresses are anonymized.
  • Contact Form: If you submit our contact form, your name, email address, and message may be stored to allow us to respond to your inquiry.

3. Cookies & Tracking (GDPR Notice)

We use cookies and similar technologies on this site:

  • Essential Cookies: Used to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) and cookie consent. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics 4 uses cookies to track anonymous usage. You may opt out at any time using Google's opt-out tool.
  • Advertising Cookies: Some third-party ad networks (used to keep this service free) may place cookies. See Google's advertising policies for details.

By clicking "Accept" on our cookie banner, you consent to the use of analytics and advertising cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

4. YouTube & Google API Services

Our tools use the YouTube Data API v3. By using our tools, you also agree to:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

You can revoke FastSave.me's access to your Google account data at any time via Google Account Security Settings.

5. Affiliate Link Disclosure

FastSave.me may contain affiliate links to third-party products or services. If you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These partnerships help us keep all tools 100% free for everyone. All affiliate links are clearly marked with rel="nofollow".

6. Data Retention & Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for analytics.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@fastsave.me.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4 — Privacy Policy
  • YouTube Data API — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google servers

8. Children's Privacy

FastSave.me is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data rights, please contact us at support@fastsave.me.
